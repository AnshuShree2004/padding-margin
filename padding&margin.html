PhysicsWallah started in 2016 as a YouTube channel founded by Alakh Pandey, a teacher from Prayagraj,Uttar Pradesh.[3][4] Together with co-founder Prateek Maheshwari, Pandey built an app catering to students aspiring for the National Eligibility cum Entrance Exam (NEET) and Joint Entrance Exam (JEE) in 2020. The PhysicsWallah app has registering 5 lakh downloads as of 2022.

In June 2022, PhysicsWallah launched its first offline center named "Vidyapeeth" in Kota, Rajasthan. PhysicsWallah also runs 45+ offline centers across 60 cities such as Delhi, Bhopal, Varanasi, Lucknow, Pune, Patna, and Ranchi.[6]
